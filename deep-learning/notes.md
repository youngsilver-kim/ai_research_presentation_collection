# Deep Learning

This directory contains study materials and presentation slides related to deep learning concepts, architectures, and training methodologies.

The focus is on building a clear understanding of how deep neural networks work, both intuitively and mathematically, while connecting theory to practical applications.

## Objectives

* Understand core deep learning architectures (CNN, RNN, Attention, etc.)
* Learn how neural networks are trained and optimized
* Develop intuition behind complex models and mechanisms
* Organize knowledge through presentation-based learning

## Topics Covered

* Neural Networks Fundamentals
* Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN)
* Recurrent Neural Networks (RNN)
* Attention Mechanisms & Transformers
* Loss Functions and Optimization
* Regularization Techniques

## Structure

Each subfolder represents a specific topic and may include:

* Presentation slides (PDF / PPT)
* Summary notes (key concepts and intuition)
* Additional references if necessary

## Learning Approach

* Prioritize intuitive understanding before formal derivations
* Break down complex architectures into simple components
* Focus on “why it works” rather than memorization
* Relate concepts to real-world AI applications
