# Probability

This directory contains study materials and presentation slides on probability theory, a fundamental component of machine learning and statistical modeling.

The focus is on understanding uncertainty, randomness, and how probabilistic thinking is applied to real-world AI problems.

## Objectives

* Understand core probability concepts and distributions
* Build intuition for randomness and uncertainty
* Learn how probability is used in machine learning models
* Strengthen the mathematical foundation for statistical reasoning

## Topics Covered

* Basic Probability Concepts (events, sample space)
* Conditional Probability and Bayes’ Theorem
* Random Variables (discrete and continuous)
* Probability Distributions (Gaussian, Bernoulli, etc.)
* Expectation and Variance
* Independence and Conditional Independence

## Structure

Each topic may include:

* Presentation slides (PDF / PPT)
* Summary notes (key concepts and intuition)
* Simple examples for better understanding

## Learning Approach

* Focus on intuitive interpretation before formal notation
* Use real-world examples to explain abstract concepts
* Emphasize connections to machine learning applications
* Reinforce understanding through repeated explanation

## Relevance to AI

Probability is essential in:

* Model prediction and uncertainty estimation
* Bayesian inference
* Loss functions and likelihood-based learning
* Generative models
